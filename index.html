What You Get

Reporting. Tracking. Handover. Intelligence.

Four capabilities that work together to give construction teams complete site visibility — from the first message of the day to the final handover pack.

📋

Daily shift reports

Every day closes with a structured summary: who was on site, what was completed, what issues were raised, and what remains open. Exportable as PDF or CSV for client and insurer records.

🔴

Live issue tracker

Every raised issue is tracked from open through acknowledged to closed. Each entry includes the original message, sender, timestamp, severity, and media evidence. Nothing disappears.

📦

Handover documentation

When the project ends, the full evidence pack is already built: timestamped entries, photo links, trade-by-trade activity logs, and manager sign-offs — ready for clients and regulators.

💬

Ask the logs

Managers ask plain-English questions about any part of the site record. "What happened on Level 3 this week?" or "List all open issues from the steel crew" — answered instantly from the ledger.
